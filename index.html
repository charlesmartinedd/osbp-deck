Video Development Journey

From concept to delivery — understanding the evolution of video production
Three-Phase Breakdown
📝 Pre-Production
~30% of total effort
  • Scriptwriting & storyboarding
  • Location scouting & talent booking
  • Equipment planning & scheduling
  • Stakeholder reviews & approvals
🎥 Production
~25% of total effort
  • On-site filming with crew
  • Lighting & audio setup
  • Multiple takes & B-roll capture
  • Director oversight on every scene
✂ Post-Production
~45% of total effort
  • Video editing & color grading
  • Sound mixing & music licensing
  • Graphics & captions
  • Review cycles & final render
